Academic Departments

Discover the curriculum, advanced labs, ongoing projects, and faculty of our core academic centers.

School of Tech

Computer Science & Engineering

The largest division at Apex, specializing in Machine Learning, Cloud Services, and Distributed Networks.

View CSE Faculty

Our CSE department offers standard course structures designed under ACM guidelines. Students gain skills in Compiler Design, OS kernels, Full-stack web technologies, and Big Data processing algorithms.

Supercomputing Wing: Houses 12 high-performance cluster servers.
Nvidia AI Innovation Lab: Equipped with RTX GPUs to process Deep Learning computer vision training datasets.

Agri-Sense: IoT sensor array mapping soil humidity in local villages using Drone imagery.
SecureChain: Decentralized document verification protocol for university transcript records.

School of Tech

Electronics & Communication (ECE)

Fostering expertise in VLSI, IoT devices, signal processing, and telecommunication hardware.

View ECE Faculty

The ECE curriculum targets embedded logic circuits, cellular communications, fiber optics, and microprocessor architectures. Practical lab work constitutes 40% of the syllabus.

VLSI Circuitry Lab: Equipped with Cadence design suites.
Antenna Testing Lab: RF chamber for measuring transmission frequencies up to 5GHz.

Bio-Band: Wearable biosensor tracking heart rate telemetry transmitting alerts to nearby doctors via Bluetooth.

School of Business

Management & Business Administration

Training analytical business leaders for Finance, Operations, Strategy, and Digital Transformation.

View MBA Faculty

The Management school focuses heavily on case study methodologies, corporate board simulations, financial market analytics, and executive communication models.

Financial Markets Lab: Real-time trading terminals simulation matching NASDAQ pricing ticker data feeds.

MarketPulse: Strategic advisory survey mapping e-commerce adoption indicators across suburban retail networks.
